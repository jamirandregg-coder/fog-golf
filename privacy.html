Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

FOG (Friends of Golf) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Data We Collect

  • Player names and golf scores (entered by the league admin)
  • RSVP responses (yes/no for weekly rounds)
  • Push notification tokens (if you opt in to notifications)
  • Anonymous authentication session (no personal info required)

How We Use Data

All data is used solely for league management: tracking scores, standings, handicaps, scheduling, and sending optional push notifications about league events.

Data Storage

Data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database (Google Cloud). We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete your session data at any time by tapping "Delete My Data" in the app footer. To request removal of your name and scores from league records, contact the league admin.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication, database, hosting, push notifications)
  • Venmo links (external payment, not processed by the app)
