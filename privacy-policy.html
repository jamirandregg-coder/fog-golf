FOG Golf League - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

FOG Golf League ("the App") is a private golf league management application for the Friends of Golf (FOG) group. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Player Names: Names of league members, entered by the league administrator, used to display rosters, scores, and standings.
  • RSVP Responses: Whether a player has confirmed attendance for a given week.
  • Golf Scores: Hole-by-hole scores entered during rounds.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens used to deliver push notifications about league updates. These are stored securely and used solely for sending league-related notifications.

How We Use Your Information

All collected information is used exclusively for operating the FOG Golf League, including:

  • Displaying weekly schedules, pairings, and tee times
  • Tracking RSVPs and attendance
  • Recording and displaying golf scores and standings
  • Sending push notifications about round status and league announcements

Data Storage

Data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database, a service provided by Google. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Database hosting and push notification delivery
  • EmailJS: Used for sending RSVP summary emails to the league administrator only

Data Retention

League data (scores, standings, schedules) is retained for historical records across seasons. Push notification tokens are automatically cleaned up when they become invalid.

Your Rights

As a league member, you can request removal of your data by contacting the league administrator. Push notifications can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the league administrator at jamirandregg@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
